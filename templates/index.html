About me

My name is Paweł, and I am a second-year Computer Science and Econometrics student at the Warsaw University of Life Sciences. I began my studies in October 2022 after graduating from high school in May of that year. From primary school, I have been passionate about computer science. Initially, I learned how to create graphics, thumbnails, and banners, and later, I became interested in web development. I tried my hand at creating dozens of different websites. I learned HTML to structure my pages and CSS to improve their visual appearance. Naturally, I also gained some knowledge of JavaScript and SASS.

At university, I learned how to program and create simple object-oriented programs in C#. I also gained knowledge in econometrics, mathematical statistics, descriptive statistics, Excel, and R. During my fourth semester, I also learned Python and SQL, which I now see as the foundation of my future career. In the future, I plan to explore libraries and technologies related to machine learning to better analyze data sets.

In May 2024, I began my university internship at KARL STORZ. I am involved in translating medical components and device sets from English to Polish. I learned how to use Adobe Acrobat and work with PDF files. I also worked with internal company data, creating pivot tables and reports. Seeing the potential for automating repetitive tasks that took a long time, I developed an application to make everyday work easier for employees. After my internship, I stayed on at KARL STORZ and continue to gain valuable experience.
